Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2025  |  LuxeStore  |  ecombuss.gt.tc

LuxeStore ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website and WhatsApp services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide when you:

  • Create an account or make a purchase
  • Contact us via WhatsApp or email
  • Place an order or track a delivery

This includes: name, email address, phone number, shipping address, and payment information.

2. WhatsApp Data Usage

When you interact with our WhatsApp Business bot, we collect your phone number and conversation messages to:

  • Respond to your order inquiries and tracking requests
  • Send order confirmations and delivery updates
  • Provide customer support

We do not use WhatsApp data for advertising or share it with third parties without your consent.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send order confirmations and delivery updates via WhatsApp
  • Communicate about products, services, and promotions
  • Improve and personalize your shopping experience
  • Protect the security and integrity of our services

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Sensitive data is encrypted via SSL/TLS technology. We do not store payment card details on our servers.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience, remember cart items, and understand site usage. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not function properly.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • WhatsApp Business API (Meta) – for customer communication
  • Razorpay – for secure payment processing
  • Google – for OAuth login

We do not sell or trade your personally identifiable information.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

8. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
